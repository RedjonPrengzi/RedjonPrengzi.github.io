PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

At The Daily Bar, we respect your privacy. We believe in mastercraft lyricism, not data mining. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The Daily Bar operates on a "Local-First" basis. This means:

  • No Personal Data: We do not create user accounts, ask for your name, email, phone number, or track your location.
  • Local Storage: Your "Crate" (Favorites), settings, and viewing history are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • No External Servers: We do not upload your usage data to external servers for processing or analysis.

Advertising and Tracking

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device Identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS or Advertising ID on Android)
  • Coarse Location data (derived from IP address)
  • Usage Data (ad views, clicks, and interactions)
  • Diagnostics (crash logs and performance data related to ads)

2. Third-Party Services

The Daily Bar uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob & Google Play Services

The app is distributed via the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. These platforms may collect anonymous usage statistics (such as crash reports or install counts) governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. Notifications

If you opt-in to the "Daily Drop" notifications, these are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use remote push notification servers to track your device token.

4. Your Rights and Choices

iOS Users: On iOS, you can limit ad tracking by going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track". You can also reset your Advertising Identifier at Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Ad Preferences: You can manage your ad preferences and opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings or via Google Ads Settings.

5. Contacting Support

If you contact us via email for support, we will only use your email address to reply to your inquiry. We do not add your email to marketing lists or share it with third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect user emails, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.
