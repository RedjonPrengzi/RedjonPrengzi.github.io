The Philosophy

Clarity is the ultimate feature.

Most apps are built to maximize engagement. Endless feeds, notifications, complexity masquerading as value. I build for the opposite: intentional products that respect your time, solve one problem completely, and get out of the way. Every feature I ship answers one question: "Does this make the core job easier?" The result is software you don't think about. You just use it.
