Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data Ownership & Storage

Fastual is built as a "Local-First" application. All your fasting data, history, statistics, and preferences are stored directly on your device using local database technologies. We do not maintain a central server to store your biological data.

What this means: Your fasting records never leave your device. You are the sole owner of your data.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We do not track your location. We do not use third-party analytics services or crash reporting tools.

The app only processes the data you explicitly enter: fasting timers, water intake, and optional mood notes.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data persists on your device as long as the app is installed. If you uninstall Fastual, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

Important: Since Fastual is local-first with no cloud sync, there is no backup of your data. If you lose your device or uninstall the app, your fasting history cannot be recovered.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may interact with Apple HealthKit or Google Fit only if you explicitly grant permission. This interaction happens strictly between the Fastual app and your device's operating system — no data is sent to external servers.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control. You can export your data at any time through the app settings, or delete individual entries. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data.

6. Contact

If you have any questions regarding privacy or how your data is handled, please contact us at prengzi.redjon@gmail.com.
