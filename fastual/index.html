Get Started in Minutes

1. Download Fastual

Available on both iOS and Android. Open the app to start tracking immediately without needing a complex setup process.

2. Set Your Fasting Window

Choose your eating and fasting hours. Start with 16:8 if you're new, or go straight to 20:4 if you're experienced.

3. Start Your First Fast

Tap start when you finish eating. The timer tracks your progress and notifies you when your window opens. That's it.
